About me

I'm GomathiBalan — a Senior Program Manager with over a decade of experience turning complex, cross-functional work into clear, delivered outcomes. I work at the intersection of people, process, and technology, helping enterprise teams move faster without losing alignment.

What inspired this project
"I'm not a developer. I'm not a writer. Turns out, that didn't matter."

I've spent my career helping developers, designers, and product teams deliver great software. I understand the tools they need, the pain points they hit, and the small utilities they wish existed but never have time to build.

At some point I started wondering: what if the prompt was the skill? I co-created a book — Coffee, Chaos & Jira — with AI. Then I built 16 browser-based tools the same way. No IDE, no terminal, no staring at a blank page. Just clear thinking, sharp prompts, and fast feedback loops.

It turns out good prompting has a lot in common with good program management: define the outcome clearly, iterate quickly, and know when to push back on the first answer you get.
